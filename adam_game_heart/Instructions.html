In this game you will have to choose between three goods.

  • For each good there are attributes.
  • Attributes are hidden within table cells.
  • You will also have the possibility to like each good.
In a nutshell, you will have to open the cells to find out more about each good.
You have the possibility to say if you like the goods as you open each cell by clicking on a heart icon.
  • The heart is empty
  • If you click, it fills
  • After 2 sec it empties again so that you can give another like.
You have 45 seconds until the matrix transposes.

Important: You can only give one like per box opened
