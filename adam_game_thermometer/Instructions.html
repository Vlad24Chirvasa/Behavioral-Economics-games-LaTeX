In this game you will have to choose between three goods.

  • For each good there are attributes.
  • Attributes are hidden within table cells.
  • You will also have the possibility to choose how much like you each good.

In a nutshell, you will have to open the cells to find out more about each good.
You have the possibility to say how much you like the goods as you open each cell.

  • The last column of the matrix has sliders like this one:

    0   100  

  • You can move the slider to choose how much you like each good
You have 45 seconds until the matrix transposes.
Important: you can only like one good per box opened
